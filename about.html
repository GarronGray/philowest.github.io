I’m moving to Sydney in Jan 2016! I have a Skilled Independent visa (subclass 189). Please feel free to Email me if you’d like to chat.

I’m a developer currently living in Joburg.

I joined Momentum (link to about page) in 2009 as a software engineer. Since then I’ve helped to improve development processes in our area by introducing dependency management, continuous delivery, and various automated testing approaches. I initially worked on our client-facing web site, and later moved to building shared services and applications for the group. Towards the end of 2012 we made the shift towards a more agile way of work, and despite the frustrations that come with doing this in a big corporate, we’ve experienced an increase in productivity along with a better work/life balance.

Prior to that I worked at Telkom, and then Discovery, after completing a Masters degree in Computer Science at Rhodes University in Grahamstown.
